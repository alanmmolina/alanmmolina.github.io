Project developed in October 2017 for submission to the III Scientific Show of Robotics and Automation of Sorocaba and Region. It consists of a detailed description of the construction of a robot for Sumo competitions using low cost materials. The prototype was built using knowledge of machining and manufacturing of mechanical parts and prototyping of electronic circuits. The purpose of the work was to acquire and disseminate knowledge in the area of mobile robotics.
